Mutual Fund Risk Levels

Understanding risk is the first step toward making informed investment decisions.
Low Risk Funds
  • Invest in government or high-grade bonds
  • Very stable with predictable returns
  • Ideal for conservative investors
Moderate Risk Funds
  • Balanced mix of equity and debt investments
  • Moderate volatility with improved growth
  • Suitable for medium-term goals
High Risk Funds
  • Invest heavily in equity markets
  • High volatility with high return potential
  • Best for long-term aggressive investors
Risk Level Investment Type Volatility Ideal For
Low Government Bonds / Liquid Funds Very Low Safe & Conservative Investors
Moderate Hybrid / Balanced Funds Medium Medium-term Planners
High Equity / Sectoral Funds High Aggressive Long-term Investors
